About

John is a Software Engineer and Data Scientist at HeavyWater working with big data and machine learning. Additionally, he is an open source maker and contributor responsible for co-creating the Heupr platform as well as a variety of other smaller projects.

He is a periodic blogger and is in the process of compiling a trilogy of a short work of science fiction he has been gradually writing over the years. In his spare time John enjoys lifting and yoga; also, he is pushing himself to read more books and is attempting to improve his chess game.

John resides in Philadelphia, PA.
